Catfield Town FC is a fantasy draft football club on football manager that played in IDFM Championship, managed by Faishal Hanif from Kediri.

Faishal Hanif mostly uses 4-2-1-3 formation
On defending sides, Faishal put 4 defender with different role, 2 fullback, 1 ball-playing defender, and 1 central defender. On midfield side, Faishal use 3 midfielder with 3 different positions and roles. Deep-lying playmaker, box-to-box midfielder, and natural central midfielder. On forward, Faishal use 2 winger with inside forward role and one striker with advanced forward role.

Why 4-2-1-3, we adopt attacting football style but always stay safe, we are set positive mentallity during the match, so we not only focusing on attacking movement but also defending movement. We are using high defensive line engagement for every match, why? because we want our player stay close / or not too far from each other so the player easily to pass. But the weakness is if the opponent using fast counter-attack, so this is why we also need strong center defender who has run ability to cover it.

On midfield, the combination DLP, BBM, and natural CM work perfectly on our team. DLP will sit in front of defender above def line engagement during attacking and sometimes help attacking. BBM is very important, he help can attack and defend perfectly so BBM is important.
